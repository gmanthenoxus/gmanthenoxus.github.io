My Story

My journey started with a simple fascination: how can we use logic to solve any problem? That question led me from mathematics to computers, and eventually to code. What began as curiosity became a passion when I realized I could build tools that actually solve real problems for real people.

Over the past 11+ years, I've built custom solutions for organizations across different industries, but something was missing. I was solving everyone else's problems while my own passions sat on the back burner. That's when the 49 Project Quest was born—a commitment to build meaningful projects across all 7 of my core interests.

Now I'm pursuing a Master's in IT with Data Science specialization, not just to level up my skills, but to bring data-driven thinking to everything I build. Whether it's tracking anime watch stats or optimizing fantasy sports lineups, I'm learning to merge technical expertise with genuine passion.

"The best projects aren't the ones that look good in a portfolio—they're the ones that solve problems you actually care about."
