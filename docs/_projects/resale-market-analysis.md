## Abstract

This research project analyzes sneaker resale markets to identify profitable investment opportunities, pricing trends, and market inefficiencies. By aggregating data from multiple marketplaces and applying financial modeling techniques, we aim to provide data-driven insights for sneaker investors and collectors.

**Status**: This project is in the planning phase. Detailed findings and analysis will be added as work progresses.

---

## Research Questions

1. What pricing patterns exist across different sneaker resale marketplaces?
2. How do release hype and scarcity affect long-term resale values?
3. What market inefficiencies can be exploited for profitable investments?
4. How can we predict future resale prices with reasonable accuracy?
5. What risk factors should investors consider in the sneaker market?

---

## Methodology

### Data Collection
- **Sources**: StockX, GOAT, eBay, regional marketplaces
- **Scope**: Historical pricing, sales volume, release data
- **Frequency**: Daily updates for real-time analysis

### Analysis Approach
- Time-series analysis of price trends
- Comparative analysis across marketplaces
- Volatility and risk modeling
- Machine learning for price forecasting
- Market sentiment analysis

### Financial Metrics
- Return on Investment (ROI)
- Price volatility
- Market liquidity
- Arbitrage opportunities
- Risk-adjusted returns
